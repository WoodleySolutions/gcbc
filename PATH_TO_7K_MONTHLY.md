- GCBC needs to gross: $X + 30% (taxes) + operating costs

  - **This is feasible by Month 18-24**

### Risk Mitigation:

**Financial Risks:**
- Don't quit Tyler's job until GCBC consistently hits $5-6K/month for 6 months
- Build 6-month emergency fund before transition
- Keep health insurance in mind (COBRA or marketplace)
- Start as side hustle, scale gradually

**Income Volatility:**
- Diversify revenue streams (not dependent on one source)

- Seasonal fluctuations (slower in summer, busy in fall)
