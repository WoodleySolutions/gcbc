## ‚öñÔ∏è Mission Preservation Principles

To monetize WITHOUT compromising your mission:

### ‚úÖ DO:
- Keep core content free (monthly discussions, basic blog, YouTube)
- Offer premium as "enhanced experience" not "required"
- Be transparent about money (where it goes, why you're asking)
- Use income to improve content quality
- Maintain authenticity (only promote what you genuinely love)
- Offer scholarships/free access to those who can't afford
- Remember: community first, revenue second

### ‚ùå DON'T:
- Paywall the monthly book discussions
- Compromise content for sponsors
- Create artificial scarcity
- Make people feel "less than" if they don't pay
- Lose the personal, authentic voice
- Burn out trying to do too much

## üö® Risks & Considerations

### Financial Risks:
- Inconsistent income (especially starting out)
- Platform fees (Patreon, payment processors take 5-15%)
- Taxes (self-employment tax, quarterly estimated taxes)
- Time investment may not pay off immediately

### Mission Risks:
- Community perception ("they sold out")
- Pressure to create more content (burnout)
- Temptation to prioritize revenue over mission
- Comparison trap (other creators earning more)

### Personal Risks:
- Less time for family
- Hobby becomes "work" (loses joy)
- Online criticism increases with visibility
- Privacy concerns as platform grows

### Legal/Tax:
- Need LLC or business structure
- Self-employment taxes (15.3% + income tax)
- Bookkeeping requirements
- Potential state sales tax on digital products

---

## üíº Business Structure Recommendations

If you pursue monetization:

**Start (< $10K/year):**
- Sole proprietorship (simplest)
- Report on Schedule C
- Track expenses carefully

**Growth ($10K-50K/year):**
- Consider LLC for liability protection
- S-Corp election possible for tax savings
- Hire bookkeeper
- Separate business bank account

**Established ($50K+/year):**
- Definitely LLC or S-Corp
- CPA for tax planning
- Possibly hire help (VA, editor, etc.)
- Professional insurance

## üéØ Bottom Line

### Can You Monetize GCBC?

**Monetize IF:**

## üìù Questions to Consider

Before making any decisions:

1. **Why?** What's your real motivation?
   - Offset costs?
   - Enable more time for content?
   - Build toward something bigger?
   - Just curious about potential?

2. **How much time?** What's realistic?
   - 5 hours/month?
   - 10-20 hours/month?
   - Could this become full-time someday?

3. **What's success?** Define it clearly:
   - Break even on costs ($100-500/month)?
   - Part-time income ($2,000-5,000/month)?
   - Replace a salary ($5,000-10,000/month)?

4. **Mission preservation**: What's non-negotiable?
   - Free monthly discussions?
   - No paywalls on main content?
   - Maintain authentic voice?

5. **Family impact**: How would this affect:
   - Time together?
   - Stress levels?
   - Long-term vision?
